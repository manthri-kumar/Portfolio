Project Overview

Why Sarathi?

Problem Statement

Most travel applications require users to search multiple websites, compare places manually, plan itineraries themselves, estimate budgets separately, and find nearby attractions individually — using five different apps for one journey.

Solution

Sarathi combines AI trip planning, day-wise itinerary generation, an intelligent travel chatbot, location-aware recommendations, saved places, and multilingual support into one seamless platform — your journey, our guidance.

Development Journey

From research to production — the complete engineering lifecycle.

Research & Planning

Market analysis identifying gaps in travel platforms — no single tool combined AI planning, chatbot, multilingual support, and map navigation.

Completed

UI Design

Premium dark interface inspired by Linear, Stripe, Vercel, Apple Wallet, and Notion — glassmorphism, soft gradients, micro-interactions.

Completed

Frontend Development

React SPA with React Router, Axios, Lucide Icons, i18next, responsive layouts, and smooth animations across all modules.

Completed

Backend Development

Node.js + Express API with 10 route modules, MongoDB Atlas, JWT auth, OTP email via Nodemailer, and Google OAuth integration.

Completed

AI Integration

Generative AI powering the Trip Planner, Day Planner, and Travel Chatbot — personalized itineraries, intelligent responses, and travel recommendations.

Completed

Deployment

Frontend on Vercel with CI/CD pipeline; backend on Render with environment-based configuration and production secrets management.

Completed
